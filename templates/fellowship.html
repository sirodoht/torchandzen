We don’t want to discriminate against poor people. For this reason, we have reserved two spots for those who cannot afford to pay the ticket fee.

To be considered, please send an email to theo+fellowship@torchandzen.com. We’d rather not judge whose poverty is worse. The process we’ll follow is as follows. We’ll read all emails. We’ll verify as much as possible of what is written to us. Then, we’ll assign numbers to each mail, go to randon.org/integers, fill in the max value, and ask for two numbers.
