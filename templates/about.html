Torch and Zen: About

Who’s organising this and why should we trust them?

Theodore Keloglou is a software engineer with almost ten years of professional experience, mostly in startups and small companies across industries such as FinTech, Bio/Health-Tech, DevTools, et al.

Over the last decade, he has organised a wide variety of events and has been on the organising committee of conferences with hundreds of attendees. He has coordinated numerous meetup groups in multiple countries, on topics such as software programming, but also photography, philosophy, civic technology.

He is a member of Chaitin School, a software engineering community in London, England, the maker of mataroa.blog, a privacy-first blogging platform, the founder of Laniakea Books, a public domain publishing house, and an employee at Animorph Co-op. His blog is at nutcroft.com
