To take part in Torch and Zen, you need to plan your time there. You will have 3 weeks. Treat them as your time budget. This will feel like making a bet. Make a bet that you will win.

FAQ

Torch and Zen will take place on
8 April – 26 April 2024 in central London, UK

How much does it cost?

£2,000 per person.

Who is this for?

This program is aimed at people who want to jumpstart making a product. It’s aimed at experienced programmers who know what to build and how to build.

How do I apply?

You apply with a plan of what you are going to build. Your plan is like a budget. It doesn’t mean it will go as planned. It does mean though that you will try to make it go as planned.

Why would I come there if I can just build at home?

We think the added advantages of our program are:

  1. community: building with others means accountability
  2. structure: consistency is crucial to high quality work
  3. space: we think having a separate space for work is important
  4. feedback: from rubberducking to quick third-party feedback
  5. ethics: we’ll help you figure out the societal impact of what you’re building
  6. zen: we hope to contribute to your inner calmness while working.

What’s with the name?

PyTorch is the most popular deep learning library. Zen is a buddhist word that has come to mean deep calmness and inner peace. Building a product and a company easily become extremely stressful things. We aim to tackle that with morning meditation, daily community lunches, evening checkouts, and many other small things that we think are meaningful.
